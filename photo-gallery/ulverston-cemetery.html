Ulverston Fallen: World War I

Ulverston Cemetery

There are 18 Commonwealth War Grave Commission records listed for Ulverston Cemetery from the Great War. Several of these records are for men who survived the war but succumbed to their injuries at a later date, these men are not listed on the Ulverston War Memorial. Some do not have Commonwealth War Grave Commission headstones but are interred in family plots according to their wishes. 10 of the fallen listed on the Ulverston War Memorial are buried in Ulverston Cemetery.

The Commonwealth War Graves Commission website also has information on the Ulverston Cemetery.
